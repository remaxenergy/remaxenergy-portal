How to use the Transaction Portal
For agents
1. Select your name and enter your 4-digit PIN to sign in.
2. If this is your first time, you cannot log in this way. Your admin will send you a setup link to create your PIN.
3. Once in, tap + New to create a transaction — enter the client name, address, and closing date.
4. Fill in contract dates on the right side — enter the acceptance date and most dates calculate automatically. Double-check the dates!
5. Check off steps as you complete them. Add notes as needed.
6. Copy the Client Link and text or email it to your client — they can see progress without logging in. The link will not change throughout the transaction.
7. Leave a message for your client anytime in the message box.
